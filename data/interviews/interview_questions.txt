Here is the leading questions used during the interviews with the PhD Students.

Problem Statement
=================
What is the specific problem that each paper tries to solve?

Datasets
========
What are the types of datasets used (e.g. scientific text, web text, or images)?
What are the domains of datasets (e.g. medical,  or computer science)?
What are the sizes of the datasets?
List the names of the datasets.

Approach
=========
What is the category of the approach used to solve the problem (e.g. deep learning, or classical machine learning)?
What is the name of the method (e.g. BERT, or SVM)?
